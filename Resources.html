Journaling

Write Your Way to Clarity

Putting words to your feelings helps untangle the noise in your head. Journaling reduces stress, builds self-awareness, and lets you track how far you've come.

You don't need to write perfectly — just honestly. Try one of these prompts to get started.

1

What is one thing that went well today, no matter how small?

2

What emotion am I carrying right now, and where do I feel it in my body?

3

What would I tell a close friend who was going through what I'm facing?

4

What is one thing I can let go of today to feel lighter?
