About CampusCare

CampusCare is a safe and welcoming mental health early support system designed to help students, counselors, and universities connect better and provide emotional support when it matters most.

"Healing is not linear, and you do not have to go through it alone."

"Your feelings are valid, your voice matters, and help is always available."

Why Was This System Built?

Many university students silently struggle with stress, anxiety, burnout, depression, academic pressure, and emotional challenges. Unfortunately, some students do not know where to seek help or feel afraid to speak up.

CampusCare was created to provide an early support platform where students can safely access counselling services, wellness resources, emotional support, and mental health assistance within their institution.

The Process

How CampusCare Works

From the first time you reach out to long after your first session, the system is built to keep support moving with you.

Step 01

Reach Out

Open your student portal and share what's on your mind, in your own words and your own time.

Step 02

Get Matched

We connect you with a counselor suited to what you're going through.

Step 03

Receive Support

Attend sessions, track how you're feeling, and explore wellness resources whenever you need them.

Step 04

Stay Supported

Your counselor and the Dean of Students' office stay informed, so support continues beyond a single conversation.

Who Does CampusCare Help?

Students

Helping students access mental wellness support, counselling appointments, and self-care resources.

Counselors

Assisting counselors in managing sessions, supporting students, and monitoring wellness needs.

Dean of Students

Giving the Dean of Students' office visibility into welfare cases that need attention across the university.

Administrators

Helping institutions strengthen student wellbeing systems and coordinate mental health response services.

Common Questions

Is what I share confidential?

Yes. Your conversations with a counselor stay private and are only shared further if there's a serious safety concern.

Does it cost anything?

No. Every student at the university can use CampusCare at no extra cost.

What if I need help right now?

Use the Emergency Contact button on the home page to reach urgent support immediately.

Can I change counselors?

Yes. If a match isn't the right fit, you can request a different counselor from your student portal.
