Our Mental Wellness Services

CampusCare provides supportive services that help students, counselors, and universities create a healthier and safer learning environment.

Counselling Appointments

Students can book counselling sessions online with university counselors safely and easily.

Mood Tracking

Students can track emotions, stress levels, and monitor their mental wellness journey.

Wellness Resources

Access articles, self-care guides, coping strategies, and educational mental health materials.

Reminders & Notifications

Receive reminders for appointments, wellness check-ins, and support activities.

Emergency Support

Quick access to urgent support services, emergency contacts, and crisis assistance.
